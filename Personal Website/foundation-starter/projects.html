This past semester I took CISC 350 and this was an assignment to check some encrypted passwords The p1hashes file contained some passwords that were encrypted and converted to hex. I then took the 10 million most common passwords and encrypted them in the same way as p1hashes was. I then converted it to hex and checked to see if they matched any of the passwords on p1hashes.

This was a project from CISC 230 where I made a program to solve Futoshiki puzzles. In this assignment I used the ForkJoinPool to break down the computational intensive task into smaller tasks that are more easily solved. This allowed me to solve the puzzle much faster than just brute forcing through the whole puzzle which is very inefficient.
